Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to this mini digital edition of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley's Frankenstein, created as a project for Text as Data at the University of Antwerp.

This project presents transcriptions of a selection of manuscript pages from the original draft, including annotations and corrections made by Mary Shelley and her husband Percy Bysshe Shelley. The novel, first published in 1818, tells the story of Victor Frankenstein and his creation, exploring themes of ambition, responsibility, and the consequences of playing god.
